# Dummy-Client Brief

## Client

- **Name:** BrightNest Cleaning Co.
- **Type:** Local cleaning service
- **Location focus:** Quezon City, Pasig, Mandaluyong, Taguig, and nearby Metro
  Manila areas
- **Primary buyer:** Busy homeowners, condo renters, small-office managers, and
  move-out clients

## Business Goal

BrightNest needs a credible public website that explains its services clearly
and converts visitors into qualified leads.

The site should collect enough information for follow-up without asking the
visitor to understand scheduling, pricing, or internal operations upfront.

## Website Goal

1. Establish trust quickly.
2. Explain the service categories.
3. Make it easy to request a quote.
4. Save each request to an admin inbox.
5. Notify the business owner through a demo-safe email outbox.
6. Let the owner move leads through simple follow-up statuses.

## Lead Fields

- Full name
- Email
- Phone
- Requested service
- Preferred contact method
- Message
- Consent to be contacted

## V1 Services

- Standard Cleaning
- Deep Cleaning
- Move-In / Move-Out
- Small Office Cleaning
- Post-Renovation Reset

## Tone

Calm, dependable, practical, local, and easy to contact.

## Non-Goals

- Appointment booking
- Online payments
- Staff scheduling
- CMS editing
- Multi-location management
- Full CRM behavior

Those are useful future surfaces, but they would blur the first showcase.
